# Review Experiment Results

Review these experiment results and provide:

1. **Key Findings**: What worked, what didn't
2. **Metric Analysis**: Trends, anomalies, statistical significance
3. **Diagnosis**: Underfitting? Overfitting? Instability? Wasted budget?
4. **Next Steps**: Top 3 highest-value experiments to try next
5. **Updated Plan**: Revise expected gains and experiment ordering based on new evidence

Think in bits, not vibes. Be specific about what the numbers mean.
